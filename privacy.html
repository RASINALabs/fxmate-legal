FX Mate — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 9 June 2026

FX Mate is built to respect your privacy and to collect as little information as possible.

1. Information stored on your device

The app saves the following ONLY on your phone, so it can reopen the way you left it: your chosen currencies, your display preferences (such as dark mode), any custom exchange rates you enter, and the most recent exchange rates it downloaded. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

2. We do not collect personal information

There are no accounts to create. We do not ask for, collect, or store your name, email, contacts, photos, or location, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Exchange rate data

When you tap Refresh, your device contacts a third-party exchange-rate provider (exchangerate-api.com, via open.er-api.com) to download current rates. The app only requests publicly available rate data and does not send them any personal information. Their handling of your network request is governed by their own privacy policy.

4. Analytics and advertising

This version of the app contains no analytics and no advertising. If advertising or a paid "premium" option is added in a future version, this policy will be updated to clearly describe the providers involved (for example, Google AdMob for ads and the app stores for purchases) before those features go live.

5. Children

The app is a general-purpose utility suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above shows when it last changed.
